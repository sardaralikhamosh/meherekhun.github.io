About Meher-e-Khun

Nestled in the serene beauty of Hundur Village, Yasin Valley, Northern Pakistan

Our Mission

Meher-e-Khun is dedicated to nurturing the vibrant musical spirit of Northern Pakistan's youth, preserving its rich musical heritage, and fostering global artistic exchange.

We provide a safe haven for budding musicians, free from societal judgment, where they can explore and develop their musical talents while reviving endangered musical traditions.

Through innovative educational initiatives and international collaborations, we ensure the evolution of these precious cultural assets for generations to come.
